Private Equity Investment Strategy

  1. Home
  2. PE Strategy

Efficient Alpha Capital’s Private Equity investment strategy is focused on partnering with growth companies in the lower-middle market. EAC’s goal is to generate superior investment returns (“alpha”), driven by top-line growth at its portfolio companies, using a more “efficient” approach compared to typical Private Equity investors. While Efficient Alpha Capital is a generalist from an industry perspective, the firm has a very specific focus with respect to a company’s business model: recurring / re-occurring revenues, large addressable market, high Gross Margins, and favorable cash flow characteristics.

Please send us a note if you are a business owner looking for a capital partner or an intermediary with a potential investment opportunity that matches EAC’s criteria above.
